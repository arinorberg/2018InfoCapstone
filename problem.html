Problem

The multifaceted nature of SightLife’s programs, and dependence on collaboration amongst remote teams, requires strong reporting systems. Before the delivery of our team’s project, SightLife collected much of their data in poorly organized Excel spreadsheets, with a lot of data being manually entered. Trends were also currently being communicated by email. SightLife needed help collecting and storing their data, as well communicating project status and key metrics within their teams, in a more streamlined and accurate way.
